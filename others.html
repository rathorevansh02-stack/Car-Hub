The Unique-Ones

This section celebrates iconic vehicles known from film, television, and animation, blending fantasy with automotive design.

Batmobile (The Tumbler)

**Origin:** Cinematic. **Design:** Military Prototype. Batman's armored vehicle, functioning as a high-speed tank and an urban assault vehicle.

Lightning McQueen

**Origin:** Pixar Films. **Type:** Piston Cup Racer. The star of the 'Cars' franchise, an arrogant but lovable NASCAR-style race car.

David-Putra 2000cc (Motorcycle)

**Origin:** Animated TV. **Engine:** Fictional 2000cc. Jack's legendary, impossibly powerful motorcycle from the cartoon 'Oggy and the Cockroaches'.

DeLorean DMC-12

**Origin:** Cinematic/Real. **Key Feature:** Flux Capacitor. Famous for its role as a time machine, and instantly recognizable by its stainless steel body and Gullwing doors.
