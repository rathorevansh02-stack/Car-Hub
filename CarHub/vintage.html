Vintage Cars Collection

Step back in time and explore the elegance, engineering, and history of iconic automobiles from the past century. This section is dedicated to the legends that paved the way for modern driving.

Mercedes-Benz 300 SL 'Gullwing'

**Year:** 1954-1963. **Design:** Iconic Gullwing doors. A masterpiece of engineering, the 300 SL was the fastest production car of its time and an enduring symbol of post-war luxury and performance.

Dodge Super Charger Concept

**Year:** 2018 (Concept/Homage). **Engine:** Supercharged HEMI V8. A modern interpretation of the muscle car era, showcasing cutting-edge power and aggressive styling, built on the Charger chassis.

1968 Dodge Charger R/T

**Era:** Muscle Car Golden Age. **Engine:** 440 Magnum or 426 Hemi. Known for its 'Coke-bottle' styling, recessed grille, and TV fame, the R/T is one of the most recognizable and powerful American classics.

Jaguar XJ13 (Le Mans Prototype)

**Year:** 1966 (Prototype). **Engine:** Mid-mounted V12. A one-of-a-kind race car, built to conquer Le Mans. Though never raced competitively, it is revered as one of the most beautiful and technologically advanced Jaguars ever built.
